If this is your first visit to our little part of the world, let me tell you about about
myself. My name is Susan Martin, and I raised beautiful American Cocker Spaniel
Puppies for sale in Niagara County, Western New York.     

Cocker Spaniels have always been my very favorite breed, and they are the only type
of puppies I raised here at High Calling Cockers.  You can see some of the precious
puppies we've raised in the past in the right margin.  You can click on the thumbnails
to view them larger.

Although many of my cocker spaniel puppies were show-worthy, I primarily raised
my cocker pups to be loving companions, to cuddle up next to you, warm your lap,
and fill your heart with love.  My time and care brought you the finest AKC Cocker
Puppy. We were committed to the highest standard of quality care.

You will see that my website is not only about my cockers.  My website reflects my
life. Cockers are a big part of it, but not the only part of it. My God, my family and
my country come first and foremost.

My puppies were all checked over by a very special veterinarian, Dr. James
Albert of All Creatures Animal Hospital in Amherst, NY. and I highly
recommend him and his wonderful clinic to anyone who has any kind of
animal at all.

All of our puppies were raised right here in my home with much love and joy!
Each one has spent much precious time in my arms and will have a special
place in my heart always and forever.

Many people visiting my website have just lost their beloved pet.  Am I
talking about "you"?   If you are hurting right now, I hope and pray you will
find the most perfect new baby to fill your heart with love and joy again.

I have collected some poems and readings written by people just like you who
know how you're feeling.  I hope they help you get through this sad time in
your lives. If you are going through a time of sadness today, please click here
to visit my Heart Songs page.

We have shown our cockers from time to time, and had a great time when we
did! I have made some truly wonderful friends alongside the show-ring!  

Raising cocker spaniel puppies is something I really enjoyed. I hope my
cocker puppies brought as much joy to your heart and home as they did to
mine!

There is nothing sweeter than a cocker puppy for your family pet! They love to please you. The AKC
Cocker Standard calls for them to be "merry", and it is a perfect word to describe these wonderful
little dogs. If you are searching for that perfect new baby, you have found your answer in a
High Calling Cocker Spaniel Puppy!

As I said above, Cocker Spaniels have always been my favorite breed!   If you are looking at this
site, I'm sure you agree!  Such precious faces, long, soft coats and sweet temperaments!  And you just
can't resist those gorgeous eyes!  In short, they are absolutely beautiful!  If you have ever had one,  
you know what I mean, and you can never, ever be without one again!
