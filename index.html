I'm currently a Web Developer for the Student Academic Success Center on campus. Previously the Webmaster for the Society of Hispanic Professional Engineers / Latinos in Science (SHPE/MAES) at CU. Society of Hispanic Professional Engineers / Latinos in Science (SHPE/MAES)

SHPE/MAES at CU Boulder chapter seeks to develop a diverse community of mutually supportive students that excel as STEM scholars, contributing community members, and well rounded professionals. We hold bi-weekly member meetings where we discuss current events in engineering and hispanic/latino education. In addition we hold professionalism workshops to better prepare our members for their careers after graduation, and encourage attendance at the national conferences hosted by SHPE and MAES.
